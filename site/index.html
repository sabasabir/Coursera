About Us

We are a startup marketing agency & cosmetic concierge, concentrating on fashion and beauty. We are in need of a writing/social media intern for our sites as well as for client sites. We are looking for a dedicated, consistent social media intern who is passionate about beauty and fashion and who is a go-getter, and can work minimum 20 hours a week virtually. The intern(s) should be relatively experienced in web development. Although this is a non-paid internship we will be able to help you get school credit and promise you will learn alot.

Job Descrption

In this role, you will operate as a skilled developer, developing on WordPress CMS and HubSpot CMS-based websites. You will be responsible for working closely with our CMO to develop custom websites and other web solutions for our company and our clients.

Responsibilities

  • Work with a team in the production of high-quality and innovative websites, built on Wordpress
  • Adhere to production schedules and work under tight deadlines.
  • Enhance skillset overtime to keep up with modern UI trends and techniques.
  • Strong knowledge and proven work experience of WordPress (we will ask for samples)
  • Good understanding of website architecture and aesthetics.
  • An ability to work on multiple projects and tasks at the same time
  • Good communication skills.

Duties

  • SEO skills
  • Understanding of web design/word press
  • Creativity
  • Website maintenance

Job Requirement

  • WordPress: 1 year (Preferred)
  • SEO: 1 year (Preferred)
  • Web Design: 1 year (Preferred)
